About

Hi, I'm Claudia.

Among many other things, I'm a software developer and graphic designer located in Columbus, OH(for the time being...there's a big world out there and I intend to explore it). I go to a little school called Ohio State University, where I'm majoring in computer science and minoring in visual communication design. Basically, I like making cool stuff with cool people, so I put together this site to house all that. So cruise around, take a look, and if you'd like to get in touch I'd love to hear from you!
